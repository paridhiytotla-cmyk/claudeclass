☿ Mercury

The closest planet to the Sun and the smallest in our Solar System.

Known as Earth's twin, Venus has the hottest atmosphere of any planet.

🌍 Earth

The only known planet that supports life and contains liquid water.

🔴 Mars

🟤 Jupiter

The largest planet with the famous Great Red Spot storm.

🪐 Saturn

Known for its spectacular rings made of ice and rock particles.

An icy giant that rotates on its side unlike any other planet.

The farthest planet from the Sun with extremely powerful winds.

Interesting Planet Facts

Hottest Planet

Venus reaches temperatures over 460°C due to its thick atmosphere.

Strongest Winds

Neptune experiences winds exceeding 2,000 km/h.

Beautiful Rings

Saturn's rings stretch over 280,000 km across space.

Largest Planet

Jupiter is so large that over 1,300 Earths could fit inside it.

Tallest Volcano

Mars is home to Olympus Mons, the tallest volcano in the Solar System.

Asteroid Belt

Millions of rocky objects orbit between Mars and Jupiter.
